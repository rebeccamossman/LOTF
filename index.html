Clark County - Leaders of the Future Cybersecurity Workshop

A hands-on cybersecurity workshop covering ethical hacking, cryptography, web and AI security and OSINT — building the fundamentals and the hacker mindset. Hosted by Clark County Cybersecurity in collaboration with C2Society and DC702.

Lesson

Ethical Hacking

The foundation — what ethical hacking is, the legal and ethical ground rules, the penetration-testing methodology, and where to keep learning (HTB, OffSec, pwn.college, and more).
Lesson

Social Engineering

Learn how attackers manipulate people rather than systems — phishing, vishing, pretexting, and how to recognize and resist these attacks in real life.
Lesson

Crypto

Cryptography — Learn about cryptography and how it drives nearly all modern security controls and technology.
Lesson

Web

Web — Learn about web hacking and the basics of how a website works (and is attacked).
Lesson

AI

Artificial Intelligence — learn what AI and LLMs are, how they're changing cybersecurity.
Lesson

OSINT

Open Source Intelligence — learn how to gather insights from publicly available information. Includes Google dorking, digital footprints, and real-world OSINT techniques.

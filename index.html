How to Get Started

Learn by Doing
  • Hack The Box
  • TryHackMe
  • pwn.college
  • OverTheWire
  • Free guided labs — no experience needed
Get Certified
  • CompTIA Security+ — entry level
  • CEH — intermediate
  • OSCP — intermediate/advanced
  • CISSP — experienced professionals
Join the Community
  • DC702 — Las Vegas DEF CON Group
  • C2Society — local workshops
  • DEF CON — world's largest hacker con, right here in Las Vegas
  • Compete in CTFs to build skills and a portfolio
Always get a parent or guardian's permission before signing up for any online platform or competition.

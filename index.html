C2Society Workshop

A hands-on cybersecurity workshop covering ethical hacking, cryptography, web and AI security, OSINT, and lockpicking — building the fundamentals and the hacker mindset. Hosted by C2Society and DC702.

About C2Society

C2Society is a cybersecurity and hacker community based in Las Vegas, Nevada. We host workshops, training sessions, and social events aimed at making cybersecurity accessible to everyone — from curious beginners to experienced professionals.

We operate in partnership with DC702, the Las Vegas DEF CON Groups chapter. DC702 has been active in the community for many years and is closely tied to DEF CON, the world's largest hacker conference held annually in Las Vegas.

C2Workshop is one of our workshops for those looking for an exposure to cybersecurity and hacking — an introduction to the tools, concepts, and mindset of our industry.
